Inheriting Wisdom, Safeguarding Health

Welcome to our treatment space. We are not only a professional treatment clinic, but also a health haven dedicated to "root-cause healing." We believe that true recovery stems from insight into and harmony with the fundamental causes of body and mind.

Our Features:

Intergration Of Ancient and Modern Methods: We combine traditional acupuncture, massage, scraping, and cupping with modern rehabilitation medicine's chiropractic osteopathy, spinal manipulation, and physical therapy to provide a more comprehensive health solution.

A Dialogue Between Body and Mind: We introduce programs such as "emotional regulation and spiritual resonance" to relieve stress and anxiety, practicing the profound wisdom of "cultivating both body and spirit."

Personalized Cycle Treatment Plans: For chronic problems and sub-health, we offer phased treatments in 3-month cycles, including regular follow-up assessments, plan adjustments, and lifestyle guidance, accompanying you to solidify your foundation and regain balance.

We specialize in: All types of acute and chronic pain, intractable pain, chronic fatigue and insomnia, women's menstrual cycle regulation, post-operative and postpartum physical reconstruction, and various sub-health conditions where "tests show no abnormalities, but physical and mental discomfort persists."

We listen attentively and analyze carefully, combining ancient wisdom with the physical and mental needs of modern people to customize a relaxed path to returning to your natural health.
